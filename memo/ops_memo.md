# Operations Memo – Retail Operations KPI Dashboard

## Summary

Implemented a structured star schema and KPI layer for retail operations using Instacart transaction data.

---

## Key Improvements

- Defined consistent fact grain.
- Built reusable dimensions.
- Introduced pricing logic for revenue simulation.
- Added QA suite to enforce data integrity.
- Delivered executive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Business Impact

- Standardized KPI definitions.
- Enabled department-level performance tracking.
- Provided visibility into retention and customer activity.
- Reduced metric inconsistencies.

## Recommended Usage
